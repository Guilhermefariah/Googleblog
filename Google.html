Google

Google is one of the largest technology companies in the world, founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Initially, it became known for its search engine, which revolutionized the way people find information on the internet. Over the years, it has expanded its operations to offer a variety of services and products, including the Android operating system, the Chrome browser, the Gmail email service, the YouTube video service, among others.

Video

Additionally, Google is a powerhouse in online advertising, especially through its advertising program, Google Ads. The company is also involved in advanced research projects such as developing independent cars and artificial intelligence. The term “google” has become synonymous with searching for information on the internet, highlighting the significant influence that Google has had on digital society.
